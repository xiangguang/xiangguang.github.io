PPPassword

Simple and safe, all data is saved locally on the user's mobile phone, no network upload is required, and network permissions are not required, so you can turn off the network permissions of this application and use it with peace of mind. It supports exporting passwords, supporting password data migration, backing up to iCloud, supporting password auto-filling, etc., and has a variety of theme colors to switch at will.
